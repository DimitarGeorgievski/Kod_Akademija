8. Flex Grow

Flex-grow controls how items expand to fill extra pace. Default is 0 (don't grow). Higher values mean the item grows more compared to its siblings.

9. Flex Shrink

Flex-shrink determines how items shrink when ther isn't enough space. Default is 1. Higher values mean the item shrinks more than its siblings
shrink:1
shrink:2

9. Flex Basis

Flex-basis sets the inital size of an item before remaining space is distributed. Can be a length (px, em) or auto (uses content size).

9. Flex Shorthand

The flex property combines grow, shrink and basis in one declaration. Common values: flex:1 (grow and shrink equally), flex:2 (grow twice as much), flex:inital (dont grow),flex: none ( fixed size).
